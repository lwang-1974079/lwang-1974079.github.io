Tour to Taiwan

Taiwan is a friendly country whose allure lies in its blend of Chinese culture with a curious fusion of Japanese, Southeast Asian and American influences.

Taiwan is a island surrounded by the ocean, so the weather is a little bit humid. However, due to its geographic location, it has rich species and different kinds of natural environment. Talking about the history of Taiwan, it is a place with enourmous cultural exchange. Taiwan had been colonized by Dutch, Portugese, Chinese, and also Japanese. Taiwan is known for its foods, and the rich culture it has. Overall, Taiwan is a must-vist country.

Taipei

Capital of Taiwan

Hualien

Located at the east side of Taiwan. You can get as close to the nature as you want it. It is the best place to be if you just want to get away from the noises and worries in the city.

Taitung

Located at the southeast of Taiwan. This is place where you can experience riding bike next to the rice field, experience the ballon festival, which it can takes you to the sky and look down the view of Taitung.

Tainan

Located in the Southwest Taiwan. This is the place with enormous historical monument and traditional Taiwanese food.
